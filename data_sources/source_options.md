# Suggested Source Plan

## Easiest option (recommended)
Use only official public sources:
1. Homepage
2. Product page
3. Pricing page
4. District/admin page
5. Blog/press page

## Why this is the easiest
- no scraping complexity
- easier to defend
- enough information for the assignment
- lower chance of unreliable data

## Medium option
Use the 5 official sources above, plus:
- one case study
- one job post
- one event/webinar page

## Harder option
Use:
- official sources
- Google News / press coverage
- LinkedIn posts
- job posts
- conference pages
- customer announcements

## Best beginner decision
Pick the easiest option first.
